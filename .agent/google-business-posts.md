## POST #3: Installation Knowledge (Mistake Prevention)
**Topic:** Common washer installation mistake causing premature roof failures

### Post Content:
Over-tightening roofing fasteners is a common installation error that compresses washers beyond design limits. This causes the metal sheet to dimple, creating stress concentration points that can tear during thermal expansion cycles. Proper installation requires tightening until the washer makes firm contact, then adding only a quarter turn. ## POST #4: Sizing Guidance (Technical Tool)
**Topic:** How to match washer diameter to sheet gauge

### Post Content:
Washer size selection depends on sheet metal thickness. Under-sized washers create excessive bearing stress, causing fasteners to pull through during wind uplift. For 0.35-0.40mm sheets, minimum 19mm diameter is recommended. For 0.50mm and above, 25mm+ umbrella washers help distribute load safely. We can assist with washer sizing calculations based on your sheet specifications and wind zone requirements. Contact our technical team in Coimbatore for project-specific selection guidance.

**Topic:** Signs your roofing washers may need replacement

### Post Content:

In moderate climates, washers generally last 12-20 years, but coastal or industrial environments may reduce this to 6-12 years.
